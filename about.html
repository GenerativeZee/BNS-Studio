BNS Studio is a contemporary practice focused on creating spaces that are modern, practical, and thoughtfully detailed.

Our Approach

We approach design as a balance between creativity and logic. Every project begins with a deep understanding of the client’s needs, followed by a structured design process that prioritizes usability, efficiency, and visual harmony.

Our philosophy is simple: Eliminate unnecessary complexity, focus on clarity and purpose, and execute with absolute precision.

Design Philosophy

Built on Principles

Refined Simplicity

We believe that simplicity is not minimal—it is refined. Our work emphasizes clean spatial planning and balanced proportions that create a sense of calm and order.

Material Honesty

We value the integrity of materials. Whether it's the raw texture of stone or the warmth of handcrafted wood, we let the materials speak their own architectural language.

Leadership

Bhavneesh Sharma

Founder & Principal Architect

With a strong focus on practical design and execution, Bhavneesh leads the studio with a hands-on approach—ensuring that every project maintains design integrity from concept to completion.

His vision is to create spaces that feel effortless yet intentional, blending contemporary aesthetics with functional longevity.
